PORTFOLIO

Quiz App

This quiz was created by using JS, specificaly jQuery as well as HTML and CSS. It consists from a starting page, seven questions of the quiz, following by a final score. When starting the quiz, the number of the particlar question and preliminary score are shown on top of the container. After answering the questions, depending on the score, the final page will display various sentences and pictures. jQuery was mainly used to (i) add event listeners, (ii) show the right question by tracking its number and hide the other questions, (iii) show and track the score to display the final score at the end, (iv) restart the quiz.
