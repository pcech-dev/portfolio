Quiz App

  • Github
  • Demo

This quiz was created by using JS, specifically jQuery as well as HTML and CSS. It consists of a starting page, seven questions of the quiz, following by a final score. When starting the quiz, the number of a particular question and preliminary score are shown on top of the container. After answering the questions, depending on the score, the final page will display various sentences and pictures. jQuery was mainly used to (i) add event listeners, (ii) show the right question by tracking its number and hide the other questions, (iii) show and track the score to display the final score at the end and (iv) restart the quiz.
